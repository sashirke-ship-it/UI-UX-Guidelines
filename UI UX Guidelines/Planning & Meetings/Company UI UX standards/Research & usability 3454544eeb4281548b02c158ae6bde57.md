# Research & usability

## When to involve users

- **New primary journey** or materially changed workflow.
- **High-risk** domains: money, health, legal, permissions.

## Lightweight methods

- **5-second test** for comprehension of key screens.
- **Task-based usability** (3–5 tasks) with 5–8 participants — enough to find big issues.
- **Heuristic review** using Experience principles + accessibility checklist.

## What to capture

- **Context**, **task**, **success criteria**, **findings**, **severity**, **recommendation**, **owner**.

## Definition of done (UX)

- Principles cited in critique; patterns/tokens used; a11y bar met; copy reviewed for glossary alignment.

## Ethics

- Consent, privacy, and inclusive recruitment practices for research.
