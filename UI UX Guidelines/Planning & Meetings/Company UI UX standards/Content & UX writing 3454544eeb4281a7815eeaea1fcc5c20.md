# Content & UX writing

## Voice and tone

- **Voice:** stable traits (e.g. clear, confident, respectful).
- **Tone:** adapts to context — errors are calm; success is warm but brief.

## Terminology and glossary

- Maintain a **glossary** of product terms (canonical vs deprecated).
- **Sentence case** for UI labels unless brand specifies title case.

## Buttons and links

- **Verb-first** button labels: `Invite teammate`, `Download CSV`.
- Links describe **destination**, not `click here`.

## Errors

Pattern: **what happened** + **how to fix** + **support path** if needed.

- Example: `We couldn't save your changes. Check your connection and try again.`

## Empty and success states

- Headline + one line of context + one primary action.

## Dates, numbers, currency

- Follow locale rules; store UTC; display local.

## Inclusive language

- Avoid idioms hard to translate; avoid gendered defaults; write at appropriate reading level.

## Microcopy for destructive flows

- Name **irreversible** outcomes explicitly; require typed confirmation for high-risk deletes.
