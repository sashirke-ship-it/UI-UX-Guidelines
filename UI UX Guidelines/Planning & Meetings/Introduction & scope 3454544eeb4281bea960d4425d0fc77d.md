# Introduction & scope

## Purpose

Establish **shared UI/UX standards** so every product team ships consistent, accessible, on-brand experiences.

## Audience

- **Product:** scope alignment and acceptance criteria for UX quality.
- **Design:** patterns, tokens, and critique references.
- **Engineering:** implementation expectations and naming parity with design.
- **Content / marketing:** voice, terminology, and in-product messaging.

## In scope

- Visual foundations (color roles, type, spacing, elevation, motion).
- Layout, responsive behavior, and design tokens.
- Core components and recurring UX patterns.
- Accessibility and inclusive language baselines.
- UX writing, errors, and empty states.
- Governance: how to propose, review, and ship changes.

## Out of scope

- Project-specific roadmaps, OKRs, or delivery plans.
- Full user-research repository (link out instead).
- **Brand guidelines PDF** — link here; do not duplicate legal/logo rules unless summarized.

## How to request a change

1. Open a thread in **Governance** or your design ops channel.
2. Describe user impact, risk, and rollout (Figma + code).
3. Steward reviews; owner approves; log in **Standards changelog**.
