# Accessibility

> **Former location:** Part III — § 11.
> 

## Accessibility baseline

- Keyboard navigable flows (including dialogs/menus)
- Visible focus states (use the **token ring** color — primary / `--ring`)
- Semantic structure (headings, labels, aria where needed)
- Contrast: ensure text and interactive elements meet contrast expectations

## Relationship to components

- Buttons and controls must document **focus-visible** behavior in Figma (see **Components & patterns**).
- Destructive / error semantics stay on **destructive** tokens — never use primary teal to communicate failure (see **Brand & foundations**).
