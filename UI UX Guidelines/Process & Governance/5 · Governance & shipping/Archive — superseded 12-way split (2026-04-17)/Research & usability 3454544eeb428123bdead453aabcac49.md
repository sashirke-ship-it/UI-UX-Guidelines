# Research & usability

## How this ties to aosenuma delivery

Use **lightweight** research to validate material UI changes — aligned with NN/g and Material as **baseline** references for sizing, density, and interaction (see CTO hub TL;DR).

## When to involve users or reviewers

- **New primary journey** or materially changed workflow.
- **High-risk** flows: money, permissions, destructive actions, bulk data edits.
- **Dense admin** vs **marketing** surfaces — validate hierarchy separately in Figma.

## Minimum methods

- **Heuristic review** against **Experience principles** + **Accessibility** baseline.
- **Task usability** (3–5 tasks) when launching a new module.
- **Visual acceptance** in Figma before treating written rules as complete (see **Governance** rollout Phase E).

## What to capture

Context, task, success criteria, findings, severity, recommendation, owner — link frames and token tables in tickets.
