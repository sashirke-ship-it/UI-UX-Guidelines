# Content & UX writing

> **Former location:** Part I — § 3 · Brand & Voice (writing rules).
> 

## Voice and writing tone

- Tone: **warm** and **accessible**.
- Default voice: first-person plural for system/product messaging where appropriate ("We…").
- Brand name must always be lowercase: **aosenuma** (including at the start of sentences).
- Prefer concise, medium-weight phrasing (confident, clear, not overly casual).

## Imagery rules

- **AI-generated images:** impressionist style, oil-painting technique.
- **Real photos:** high resolution; emphasize close-ups; avoid showing faces of people outside the company.

## UI messaging alignment

- Error copy pattern: **what happened** + **how to fix** + **how to recover** (see Layout & grid → Forms).
- **PR / review:** copy tone matches brand (warm, concise, aosenuma lowercase) — see **Release review checklist**.
